### Three boundary statements (must survive future tooling)

  pre_convention ≠ clean
    A pre_convention record means "rule not applicable", not "no problem here".
    The underlying activity is real. The obligation could not have been met
    because the obligation files did not yet exist.

  gap_observed ≠ confirmed gap
    gap_observed is a syntactic observation within a reconstruction window.
    Requires human review before promotion to gap_confirmed.
    Cannot be used as causal assertion.

  batch scan ≠ historical pattern proof
    A batch scan over N dates establishes observation records.
    It does NOT establish: frequency, trend, contributor behavior, or
    systemic governance failure. Those claims require a different evidence class.
